E-books

E-books, is convenient to get and read. It also help us, as human-beings to save the forest on the earth. In 2021, there are 900 million of hard-copy books sold on American marke, which equals to 36 million trees (Source: The Conversation). These tree can make up a 600 thousands acres of forest, which is about the size of 1 Yosemite National Park.

Currently, the trees that we grow is way less than the trees we cut down yearly. This make the trees on our earth decrease significantly. To protect our earth, we need to cut down less trees. E-books can make great contribution to this process! Switching to e-books can make a big impact to foreast protection and our future on the earth. We recommend you to use the calculator below to see how many trees are required to print a certain amount of books. And you will see how large the impact it will be to switch from hard-copy books to e-books.

Here are some advanced mathematics about trees and forest:
--> 1 standard pine tree (with 45ft usable trunk) = 10000 sheets of book paper
--> 50 trees = 1 acres of forest
--> 1 book = 200-400 papers (on average)

This calculator will display the nubmer of trees needed to produce certain amount of hard-copy books. Besides, it will also show you how many acres of forest these trees are equal to.
